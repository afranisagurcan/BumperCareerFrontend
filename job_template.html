Who are Bumper?

Please allow us to blow our own trumpet for a minute... We've built a customer-centric product that is a market leader, and we are smashing it!

We're a multi-award-winning digital payments platform within the automotive industry. We work with over 4,000 automotive retailers, helping drivers to pay for motoring costs, accessories and other services through a variety of different payment options.

Bumper is a fast-growing payments scale-up and we’re excited to continue building on our growth having recently completed a successful Series A funding round. We’re looking to hire a dynamic and passionate People Administrator, based in Ankara Turkey.

With offices in Sheffield and London in the UK, our incredible Engineering team is based in our Ankara office in Turkey. We've recently brought on new retailers in Germany, Spain and the Netherlands on board, and plan to expand further into Europe and beyond - world domination here we come!

A bit about the role...

The People Administrator, based in our Turkish office, will play a vital role in supporting the Engineering team with office duties, as well as supporting with People administration for this team. The People Administrator will also help with the HR administration of Bumper globally where required.

Reporting directly to the Chief People Officer based in the UK, you will collaborate closely with the People & Talent Partner to ensure the seamless implementation of HR processes and initiatives tailored to the local environment.
