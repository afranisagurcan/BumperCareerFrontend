"WE HELP DRIVERS STAY ON THE ROAD, WITHOUT BREAKING THE BANK."

Back in 2016, our founders James and Jack, set out to solve a very real problem; being landed with a big, unexpected car repair bill. Today we're a team of over 70 colleagues, spread across several different countries, all working towards the same goal. We help drivers stay on the road, without breaking the bank. As a multi-award-winning digital payments platform for the automotive industry, we partner with over 4,000 automotive retailers helping drivers pay for motoring costs, accessories and other services through a variety of different payment options. In January 2022, we completed our Series A funding round and established ourselves as a rapidly scaling start-up in the fast-growing Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL) market which is worth over $90 billion in Europe alone.

To be the world’s largest automotive payments platform.

To build payment solutions that enable our partners to help keep drivers safely on the road, whilst improving their profitability and customer experience.

Along with our Vision and Mission, our Guiding Principles help form the way we work - how we behave, communicate, make decisions, and work with each other.

BUMPER RETREAT - COSTA BRAVA 2023

One of the great perks of joining the Bumper family is getting to take part in our annual Bumper Retreat.

In 2023, we jetted off to sunny Costa Brava for 3 days of sun, sea and strategy! All teams from the UK, Spain, Germany, the Netherlands and Turkey came together on the first morning for our monthly Company Update, before heading out to either have a cycling tour of the area, kayaking in the sea, or a wine tasting trip! The following day was spent chilling by the pool, exploring the town, or relaxing on the beach, and finished with a surprise Catamaran trip, complete with a DJ, bar, and a swim in the Balearic Sea.

These trips are a great opportunity for teams from across the world to come together to collaborate and celebrate our success!

AUTOMOTIVE 30% CLUB

We've joined the Automotive Thirty Percent Club which has the purpose of achieving a better gender balance within the automotive industry, and with the aim of filling at least 30% of key leadership positions in the member organisations with diverse women by 2030 through a “30 by 30” strategy.

DRIVING PRIDE

We're a member of Driving Pride , a network of automotive companies working to promote LGBT+ inclusivity across the industry. The organisation aims to provide a safe and supportive space for LGBT+ people within the automotive industry, by running events for employees, sharing resources for companies to improve inclusivity, and providing a forum for employees to discuss professional development.
